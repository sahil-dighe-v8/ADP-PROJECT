What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science focused on creating machines capable of performing tasks that require human intelligence. These include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and language understanding.

Purpose of Artificial Intelligence

The main goal of AI is to develop systems that can make intelligent decisions independently. AI aims to simplify complex tasks, increase efficiency, and reduce human effort through automation and innovation.

Types of Artificial Intelligence

  • Narrow AI: Designed for specific tasks (e.g., voice assistants like Siri or Alexa).
  • General AI: Possesses the ability to perform any intellectual task a human can do.
  • Super AI: More intelligent and capable than humans, currently only theoretical.

Applications of Artificial Intelligence

AI is being used in various industries, including healthcare, education, finance, and transportation. Some key applications include:

  • Self-driving cars and drones
  • Virtual assistants (Alexa, Google Assistant)
  • Medical diagnosis systems
  • Smart recommendations (Netflix, YouTube)
  • Facial recognition and security systems
